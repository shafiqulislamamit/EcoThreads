Our Mission & Vision

What drives EcoThreads forward every day.

Our Mission

To transform Bangladesh’s textile waste into valuable fashion and lifestyle products through ethical upcycling, empowering artisans, reducing landfill waste, and promoting a sustainable circular economy.

  • Enable fair livelihoods for small makers and artisans.
  • Reduce waste through innovative reuse and recycling.
  • Build global awareness around circular fashion.

Our Vision

To be South Asia’s leading circular-fashion platform, inspiring industries worldwide to adopt waste-free production models and conscious consumerism.

  • Expand circular fashion practices to all major garment hubs.
  • Foster innovation through community-driven design and research.
  • Create measurable environmental and social impact globally.
